The Opportunity

Why Digital Marketing
for A.A.R.K Medex?

The Indian online pharmacy market is projected to reach ₹1 lakh crore by 2030. Customers are actively searching for trusted medicine sources on Instagram, Facebook, and Google. Without a strong social media presence, A.A.R.K Medex is leaving massive revenue on the table.

01

Brand Trust & Authority

People trust pharmacies they see frequently. Consistent content on Meta platforms builds brand familiarity, credibility, and customer confidence in every medicine purchase.

02

Direct Order Generation

Meta Ads can target people actively searching for specific medicines, health conditions, or wellness products — driving them directly to aarkmedex.com product pages for immediate purchase.

03

Educate & Convert

Health education content (what medicines treat what, dosage info, wellness tips) positions A.A.R.K Medex as a knowledgeable authority, increasing organic reach and trust before the sale.

04

Retarget Website Visitors

Meta Pixel on the website allows us to retarget anyone who browsed products but didn't order — recovering lost sales with personalized ads showing exactly what they viewed.

05

International Reach

Since A.A.R.K Medex ships globally, Meta Ads can target buyers in the UAE, UK, USA, Africa, and Southeast Asia — expanding beyond the Indian domestic market at scale.

06

Competitor Edge

Most local pharma brands have weak social presence. Strong, consistent creative content gives A.A.R.K Medex an immediate competitive advantage in the digital health space.

How We Work

Our Month-by-Month Process

Here's exactly how we execute your digital marketing each month — from strategy to reporting.

W1

Week 1 — Strategy & Content Calendar

We build a detailed 30-day content calendar with post themes, ad angles, caption copy, and visual briefs. Every piece is aligned with your product categories, seasonal health moments, and campaign goals.

W2

Week 2 — Creative Production

Our design and video team produces all posters, AI videos, UGC-style reels, and ad creatives. You receive everything for review and approval before any live publishing or ad spend.

W3

Week 3 — Publishing & Ad Launch

All organic posts go live on schedule. Meta ad campaigns are set up with precise targeting — age, location, interest, behavior, and custom audiences from your website visitors and customer list.

W4

Week 4 — Optimization & Reporting

We monitor campaign performance daily, pause underperforming ads, scale winners, and provide a full monthly report with reach, engagement, clicks, leads, and ROAS breakdown.
